Snickerdoodle Cookies

By: Pooja

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 ½ cups sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons cream of tartar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons sugar (for coating)
  • 2 teaspoons cinnamon (for coating)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Mix butter and sugar until creamy. Add eggs and blend well.
  3. In another bowl, whisk flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, and salt.
  4. Combine wet and dry ingredients until smooth.
  5. Mix cinnamon and sugar in a small bowl for coating.
  6. Roll dough into balls, then roll them in cinnamon-sugar mix.
  7. Place on a baking sheet and bake for 8–10 minutes until edges are golden.
  8. Cool for a few minutes, then enjoy!

Cooking time: 10 minutes

Serves: About 24 cookies
