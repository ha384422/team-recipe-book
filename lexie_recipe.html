Homemade Fortune Cookies

By: Lexie Dallison

Ingredients:

  • 2 eqq whites
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 Tbsp water
  • 1 Teaspoon almond extract

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Bat egg whites until frothy peaks form
  3. Add flour, sugar, and almond extract
  4. Whisk until completely incorporated
  5. Stir in water
  6. Prepare baking sheet
  7. Pour 1 Tablespoon onto baking sheet. Spread into a 4" or 5" circle
  8. Pour two to three circles per baking sheet
  9. Bake for five minutes
  10. After removing from oven do not let cool!
  11. Remove one cookie from the baking sheet and place a paper fortune across the center. Bring two sides of cookie together then fold cookie over edge of cup.
  12. Lastly place the cookies in empty cupcake tray to help hold the shape.
  13. Repeat until batter is gone

Cooking time: 7 minutes

Servings: 12 cookies
