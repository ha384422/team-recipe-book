Chocolate Chip Cookies

Soft, chewy, and full of chocolate chips!

Ingredients

  • 2¼ cups flour
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • ¾ cup melted butter
  • ¾ cup brown sugar
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 1 egg + 1 yolk
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1½ cups chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix dry ingredients in one bowl, wet in another.
  3. Combine both, add chocolate chips.
  4. Scoop dough onto baking sheet.
  5. Bake 10–12 minutes until golden edges.

Tip: Chill dough 20 minutes for thicker cookies!
